Description:
PhotoSweep helps you clean your camera roll without losing the photos that matter.

Swipe right to keep a photo. Swipe left to mark it for deletion. PhotoSweep keeps the process simple, fast, and private so you can review clutter, screenshots, duplicate photos, and videos without digging through your whole library manually.

Key features:

- Swipe through your photo library quickly
- Mark unwanted photos for deletion
- Review every marked item before deletion
- Find duplicate photo sets and keep one copy
- Jump ahead through nearby photos when you already know what to keep
- Zoom into photos while reviewing
- Filter by photos, videos, screenshots, and more
- Optional tilt-to-swipe controls
- On-device scanning with no photo uploads from PhotoSweep

PhotoSweep does not bypass iOS deletion protections. Photos are queued first, and iOS may ask for final confirmation before deleting anything.

PhotoSweep Pro is a one-time Lifetime Pro upgrade that unlocks unlimited cleanup access after the daily free swipe limit. No subscription is required.
