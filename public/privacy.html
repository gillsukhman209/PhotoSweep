Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2026

PhotoSweep is designed to help you review and clean up your photo library while keeping your photos on your device.

Photos and Videos

PhotoSweep requests access to your photo library so you can review photos, find duplicates, and choose items to delete. Your photos and videos are processed on your device. PhotoSweep does not upload your photo library to our servers.

Deletion

PhotoSweep marks items you choose for deletion and uses Apple Photos APIs to request deletion. iOS may show a confirmation before deleting items.

Subscriptions and Analytics

PhotoSweep may use Superwall and Apple purchase services to present subscription offers, process purchases, restore purchases, and understand paywall performance. These services may process device, purchase, and interaction data needed to provide subscriptions and analytics.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@photosweep.app.
